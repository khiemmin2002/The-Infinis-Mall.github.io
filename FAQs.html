FAQs

Is everything at Mall of America currently open?

Mall of America retail stores, attractions and dining options are open. Please check our directory page for an updated list.

What are Mall hours?

Current retail hours are Sunday-Thursday from 11:00 am to 7:00 pm and Friday + Saturday from 10:00 am to 9:00 pm. You can also check the hours page. As a reminder, guests are required to wear a face covering during their visit. Children under 5 years old are exempt.

When are Mall doors open for the public to enter?

Mall doors open three hours prior to when retail opens.

Are mall walkers allowed to walk the Mall before retail hours?

Yes. Mall walkers are welcome, but – like all guests – are required to wear a face mask that fully cover the mouth and nose. Walkers must also practice social distancing.

Are food courts open?

Yes, our food courts, fast casual, full-service and fine dining restaurants are open. You can always visit our dining page for a complete list.

Are you giving out free face masks?

Guests are required to bring and wear personal face masks with them when visiting Mall of America.

Can I still enter Mall of America if I forgot my face mask?

No, you will not be able to enter Mall of America without a face mask
